**Per-Exchange Research (from 01-RESEARCH.md):**

Binance:
- Account model: Separate margin account (cross margin)
- Auto-borrow: `sideEffectType=AUTO_BORROW_REPAY` on margin order via `/sapi/v1/margin/order`
- Transfer: REQUIRED -- `TransferToMargin` (UMFUTURE_MARGIN) and `TransferFromMargin` (MARGIN_UMFUTURE)
- Market BUY: Uses `quoteOrderQty` (USDT amount), not `quantity`
- Blackout: None known

Bitget:
- Account model: Separate cross margin account
- Auto-borrow: `loanType=autoLoan` / `autoRepay` / `autoLoanAndRepay` on `/api/v2/margin/crossed/place-order`
- Transfer: REQUIRED -- `usdt_futures` <-> `crossed_margin` via `/api/v2/spot/wallet/transfer`
- Market BUY: Uses `quoteSize` (USDT amount)
- Passphrase: Requires `BITGET_PASSPHRASE` in addition to API key/secret

**Critical pitfall (Pitfall 2 from Research):** Binance and Bitget have separate margin accounts. Spot margin orders will fail with "Insufficient margin" if funds are in futures account. The engine may need to call `TransferToMargin` before entry for these exchanges. Check if `ManualOpen` or `executeBorrowSellLong`/`executeBuySpotShort` handles this.
